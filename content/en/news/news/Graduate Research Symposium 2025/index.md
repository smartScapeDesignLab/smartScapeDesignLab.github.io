SmartScape Design Lab participated in the NUS Department of Architecture Graduate Research Symposium 2025, held on 14 November 2025 at SDE4, National University of Singapore. The annual symposium provides a platform for PhD students to share their research with the broader scholarly community, welcoming researchers and faculty from institutions across Singapore and beyond.
