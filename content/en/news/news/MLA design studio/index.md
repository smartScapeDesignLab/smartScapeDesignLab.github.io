Welcome to the MLA design studio review and exchange session, held at the NUS Department of Architecture on 7 March 2025. The session brought together students, tutors, and reviewers for an engaging conversation around landscape architecture, urban environments, and design research. Through the presentation of studio projects, students shared their ideas, site readings, and design strategies, opening up discussions on how landscape design can respond to climate, ecology, public space, and everyday urban life.
