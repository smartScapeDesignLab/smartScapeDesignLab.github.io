##  Research Direction

This research direction focuses on AI- and physics-informed approaches for urban heat mitigation, with the aim of supporting automated and evidence-based decision-making for thermally comfortable and climate-resilient built environments.

##  Output

AI-assisted decision-making frameworks, multi-objective optimisation workflows, and urban heat mitigation strategy assessment methods.
