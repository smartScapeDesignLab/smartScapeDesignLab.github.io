##  Research Direction

This research direction develops quantitative methods for assessing landscape visual quality and visual comfort, with a focus on translating 3D landscape structures and spatial characteristics into measurable design evidence.

##  Output

3D spatial metrics, point-cloud-based landscape assessment methods, and visual quality evaluation workflows for evidence-based landscape design.
