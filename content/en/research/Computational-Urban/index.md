##  Research Direction

This research direction explores how computational methods, AI, and tailored design technologies can be integrated into urban and landscape design thinking to support more efficient, transparent, and evidence-based design processes.

##  Output

Computational design workflows, AI-assisted spatial organisation strategies, and research-based design support methods for urban and landscape environments.
