title: "A Multi-Objective Parametric Framework for Green Wall Allocation at Kent Vale, Singapore"

author: Chen Jiaying

summary: |  
  This project investigates how green wall distribution in residential architecture can move beyond visual greening toward occupant-centred environmental optimisation. Using Kent Vale as a case study, it evaluates facade orientation, daylight access, thermal comfort, noise considerations, plant types, transmittance, and cost, then develops a parametric optimisation workflow with Grasshopper, Honeybee, and Wallacei to allocate modular green wall systems across residential facades.
