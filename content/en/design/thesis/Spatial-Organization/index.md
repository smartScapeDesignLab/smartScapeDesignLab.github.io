title: "AI-assisted Spatial Organization Strategies for Campus Green Space Design"

author: Chen Zhuoran

summary: |  
  This project explores AI-assisted spatial organisation strategies for campus green space design at South China Agricultural University. It responds to low accessibility, limited activity spaces, slope conditions, abandoned buildings, and simplified vegetation structure by building a design pipeline that combines dataset construction, OpenCV-based semantic vector extraction, GAN-based layout generation, Grasshopper parametric modelling, and AI-assisted layout optimisation.
